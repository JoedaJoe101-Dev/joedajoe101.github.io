The Rising Tyrant

This was a small game I created for a collage assignment (it's not very good) that I've created in RPG Maker XP

System Requirements

OS: Windows 98/98SE/ME/2000/XP/Vista/7/8/8.1/10/11
CPU: 800MHz Intel Pentium III or Higher (Recommended: 1.5GHz Pentium 4 or higher)
RAM: 128MB (Recommended: 256MB)
Graphics: Most potatoes will work
Storage:Less than 32MB

Prerequisites

A copy of RPG Maker XP RTP is required to play this game

This can be downloaded for free from the RPG Maker Website
